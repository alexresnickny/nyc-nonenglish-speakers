Non-English Speakers in NYC

New York City is one of the most linguistically diverse cities in the world. It is almost impossible to walk the streets of any part of the city without taking note of the many linguistic communities that call the five boroughs home. This map shows a snapshot in time: highlighting where non-English speakers were concentrated as of 2018.
Click on census tracts on the map to see its estimated total population and, non-English speaking population.
Data for this map comes from the Census Bureau and was accessed via the Pratt Neighborhood Data Portal.
